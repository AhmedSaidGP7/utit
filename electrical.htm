Electrical Construction - Transformers

Power distribution transformers are regularly supplied and installed by UTIT with ratings between 50KVA to 5000KVA and voltage ratios to suit the electrical distribution or generation requirements. Transformers can be supplied as free standing units or have an 13.8kV ring main unit or circuit breaker mounted directly onto the HV bushings. On the LV side fused or ACB distribution cabinets can be supplied tailoring the transformer package to the individual site requirement. At primary voltages above 13.8kV UTIT can supply and install Power transformers at voltage levels up to 33 kV for ratings between 5 and 30MVA.

Electrical Construction - Switchgear

Installation of MV voltage switchgear is a highly specialized activity and UTIT have highly specialized team to install switchgear 400V, 11KV, 13.8kV and 33KV switchgear including oil, air, SF6 and vacuum circuit breakers from 400V to 33kV. Once installed each switchboard is tested and commissioned to exacting standards.

Electrical Construction - Protection & Control Schemes

The selection of the correct protection and control scheme is fundamental to the operation of electrical distribution systems under normal and fault conditions. Our engineers understand this and are able to design the optimum system to interface between all the components of an individual site. The following protection schemes can be installed by UTIT. Over current and earth fault Pilot protection and line differential protection for cables. Busbars differential protection. Transformers differential protection and physical protection. Phase sequence and phase failure protection. Trip circuit supervision Over & Under voltage Protection. Auto reclosure Intertriping Motor protection and Motor softstarters, VFDs. Blocking and back tripping. Protection is either specified on switchgear or mounted within custom built protection panels. Protection relays can be supplied from any of the major manufacturers to provide the correct protection functionality in each individual case. Once installed all relays are tested and commissioned on site to prove correction operation of the scheme.
